Porygon2

Porygon2 is a multi-function Discord bot written from scratch over the course of 5 years (2015-present). It was created originally to serve a small gaming community (/r/MysteryDungeon). Its primary server shifted to /r/Pokemon, where it remained the primary bot for 4 years, at one point serving a community of 100,000 users. It serves moderation and managerial purposes, as well as memes.

HockeyBot

HockeyBot is a small bot that I wrote during the fall of 2016 for a friend in a hockey-related server. It accesses an open endpoint for NHL scores, parses the data, and feeds back scores as they happen to a chatroom.
